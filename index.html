About Us

We can help you create the perfect garment for any occassion. Our process is simple and straightforward. First, you'll choose your gabric from wide selection of high-quality options. We offer everything from lightweight cotton of luxurious silk, so you're sure to find something that suits your style and budget.


Next, you'll work with one of our experienced tailors to design your kaftan. We'll take detailed measurements to ensure a perfect fit, and we'll work with you every step of the way to make sure you're completely satisfied with the design. Once we have your design and measurements, we'll get to work crafting your kaftan. Our tailors are highly skilled and use only the best materials to create your garment. We will keep you update on the progress of your order and let you know when it's ready for shipping.


In addition to Yakasai clothing brand, we also offer a range of accessories to complete your look, we have everything you need to make your statement.
